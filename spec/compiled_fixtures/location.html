Location

Address

Church of the Pilgrims br 2201 P St. NW, Washington, DC 20037

Church Entrance

CitySwing DC dances are located in the basement of the Church of the Pilgrims Fellowship Hall.

Parking

Free parking is available on most roads after 6:30pm on most roads around the Church. Suggested areas are highlighted.

Metro

The closest station is Dupont Circle Station on the red line. Take the P St. exit.

Bus lines

  • G2 (Georgetwon Univ to Howard Univ)
  • D2 & D6 (Palisades/Sibley Hospital to Stadium Armory)
  • 42 (Mt Pleasant to Metro Center)
  • the N bus to Friendship Hts
  • the S bus line (Silver Spring to downtown Federal Triangle)
  • DuPont Circle Circulator from K St & Georgetown.
